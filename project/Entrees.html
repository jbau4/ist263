Entrees

Define Entrees...

To me, entrees are complete meals. These are full encompassing dishes that are plated with a main dish, a side dish, and vegetables. I like to call entrees "well rounded" meals... but lets be honest, we just love how much food is on our plate!


Crispy Rice with Peanute Noodles

You will crave this dish. This dish will appear in your dreams. Resting on a ceramic plate is crisypy nigiri with spicy tuna atop, cold peanut noodles, and salty steamed edamame.

Rice Ingredients

  • Sushi rice
  • Sugar
  • Rice vinegar or white wine vinegar
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Sesame oil
Spicy Tuna Ingredients
  • Sushi grade ahi tuna
  • siracha
  • Mayonaise | Chef Reccomends Kewpie Mayo
  • Sesame Oil
  • Chives
  • Pepper
