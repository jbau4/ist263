Tomato Pesto Grilled Chicken Sandwich on Ciabatta

This sandwich is a personal favorite. It is an original creation that is perfect for lunch. It encompasses the savory mouth watering flavors of italy with the freshest tasting ingredients.


Ingredients

  • Ciabatta bread
  • Chicken breast
  • Fresh Mozzerella
  • Tomato Pesto | Make it fresh or store bought works fine
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • Ground basil
  • Fresh basil
  • Olive oil
  • Roasted red peppers
Directions
  1. Pre-heat your oven to 375 degrees
  2. While your oven is heating, pound your chicken breast to a desired flatness
  3. Season your chicken with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and ground basil
  4. Heat up a pan with olive oil and cook your chicken for 4-5 minutes on each side.
  5. While your chicken cooks, prepare a baking tray and add your ciabatta to it with each half facing up.
  6. Lightly drizzle olive oil on each side of the bread and put it in the oven for 3 minutes once finished pre-heating.
  7. Once you pull out the bread, flip your chicken.
  8. Add 4 or more slices of Mozzerella to one piece of the bread and add your tomato pesto to the other. Put the bread back in the oven for roughly 5 minutes or until cheese is melted.
  9. Once chicken is fully cooked set aside to rest
  10. Once the cheese is melted, take out your bread, lay the chicken atop the cheese, add two or more thin slices of mozzerella on top of the chicken, close the sandwich and put back into the oven for 5 more minutes or until cheese melts.
  11. After 5 minutes is up, take out the sandwich, add roasted red peppers, fresh basic, a drizzle of olive oil, and salt &pepper. Lastly, cut the sandiwch in half, admire that crunch, and dig in!
