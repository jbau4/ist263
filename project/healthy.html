Healthy

Eat right while it is still a delight!

Eating healthy doesn't have to be a drag. You can still eat a high volume amount of food while having it be delicious. You don't need to eat a salad everyday to lose weight. I have used some of these delicious rice bowls to shed some fat.

Chefs Reccomended

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • White rice
  • Fried eggs
  • Caramelized onions
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • sweet corn
  • sliced avocado
  • chipotle sauce
  • parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Season your steak with salt and pepper and place firmly on a piping hot pan with olive oil.
  2. Cook your rice by adding to a pot with water, bringing to a boil, lowering the heat to simmer, and cooking for 15-20 minutes before removing the heat and letting it rest for 10 minutes.
  3. Once the steak is done, remove and rest. Then add your sliced onions to the same pan and add a drizzle more olive oil. Season the onions with salt and pepper and be sure to mix so they absorb all of that oil from the steak.
  4. While the onions are cooking, fry your egg. (Chef prefers over easy).
  5. Add your rice to a bowl, your steak on top, then your fried egg.
  6. Now finish by added sliced cherry tomatos, sliced avocado, your cooked onions, your canned sweet corn, store bought chipotle sauce, and a sprinkle of parmesan cheese.
